Product overview

Our Business Mortgage product is a short-term bridging and private lending solution secured against residential or commercial property. Designed for speed and certainty, it provides fast access to capital where traditional lenders cannot move quickly enough. Ideal for urgent settlements, auction purchases, equity release pending sale or refinance, and short-term debt resolution, this product delivers flexible structures, clean pricing, and same-day settlement capability (subject to security).

Who it's for

Common eligibility parameters (may vary by scenario).

Companies, Trusts (with corporate trustee), Individuals with an ABN for business purposes (non-NCCP).

Loan size

1st Mortgage: $50,000 up to $2,000,000

Property location

Category A (capital city metro & major regional centres)

Monthly, interest-only; repayment source identified (e.g., rent, business cash flow).
